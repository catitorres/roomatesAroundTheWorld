ABOUT US

About R.A.W


We believe that it is important to feel comfortable, safe and at home with the people you choose to share your days, that someone who started as your roommate becomes your family.
R.A.W is about finding those people, who make sharing awesome.

FAQs


  • Safety

    • Is very important for us that you feel safe, that's why we have people working 24/7 confirming identities and adds.

  • Contact your potential roommates

    • We offer the possibility of arranging visits to meet your flatmates face to face, or if you do not have the possibility to make it possible, you can do it online.

  • Get help

    • In our contact section you will be able to conect with our team in case you need help to understand the world of sharing homes, if you have never done so.

Our history


R.A.W was born in 2021 with the aim of creating the largest network of roommates in the world focus on young students and / or workers who wish to settle in different parts of the world.
A place where you can connect with people from the country you want to travel to safely and for free.
